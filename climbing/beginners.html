New To Bouldering?

If you are a new to bouldering, you can either come on to one of our inductions or you can go with a friend thats experienced in bouldering.

ADULT INDUCTION

Inductions are for adults who are new to bouldering and would like to use our centre. During our induction you'll learn how to keep yourself and others safe when climbing.
