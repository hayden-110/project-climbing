UNDER 18'S

Children from the age of 4 can climb. An adult can bring 2 children if they are registered with us, have previous bouldering experience, and can safely supervise their child. Our instructed sessions start at the age of 7 and up, look at the junior sessions we offer.

JUNIOR CASUAL SESSION

Ages 7-13 • Suited for Beginners • 1 Hour • £12

Junior Casual Sessions are. During the session the group will play some warm up games and exercises, they'll take part in a safety briefing before they climb. The Instructor will then supervise them climbing. We run these sessions on weekends and in the holidays. Due to the session being short, it is important that you arrive to the centre on time. There are places for the parents/carers to spectate, however they won't be allowed in the climbing area.

Book

JUNIOR NIBAS CLUB

Ages 7-17 • All Abilities Welcome

Junior Clubs follow the NICAS Bouldering scheme, it has 5 levels which take young climbers from beginners to young athletes. They run alongside the school's half terms. The sessions are 1 1/2 sessions long which run in the evenings. See the calendar for pricing, as it depends on the length of the half term.

Book

JUNIOR INDUCTION

Ages 14-17 • All Abilities Welcome • £20

Junior Inductions are for young climbers that would like to use the centre without supervision. During the session the climbers will be shown how to use the centre safely. Afterwards, they’ll need to go on a junior sign-off before the climb on their own, this is an assessment of they’re competency.
